🌡️ Why HK Climate Breaks Formulas
  • Water Film: 80%+ RH creates sweat/sebum film. Glycerin competes for water, causing severe tackiness.
  • Emulsion Stability: 30°C heat accelerates PEG hydrolysis; O/W breaks.
  • Spreadability: High humidity drops surface tension; polar oils take 3-5x longer to spread.
// HK 6-Variable Rules
  • ✓ Emo Use: Isohexadecane, Isoamyl Laurate, Jojoba Esters, Phenyl Trimethicone. ✗ Avoid Mineral oil, Olive oil, high Shea.
  • ✓ Emu Use: Wax esters (Emulium), Polyglyceryl, Aristoflex AVC. ✗ Avoid High HLB PEGs.
  • ✓ Hum Use: Betaine 2%, Ectoin 0.5%, 1,3-PDO 3%, Trehalose 1%. ✗ Avoid Glycerin >3%.
  • ✓ Oil Use: Volatile silicones, fermentation squalane. Rule: Serum oil ≤5%, Cream 8-12%.
  • ✓ Rhe Use: Carbopol Ultrez 30 (high shear-thinning), Laponite. ✗ Avoid Xanthan gum.
  • ✓ Sen Use: KSG elastomers, Boron Nitride 0.8%, Mod. Tapioca Starch 1%.
